Baltaria

Baltaria is approximately 50% of the size of Australia. It is a nice place.

Baltarias is run by 4 leaders and is considered and Oligarchy. The positions were originally Bloodline, but have since become muddled, be it retirement or abdication, the four leaders are currently Whitemane, Cassandra, Ise Kandar, and Atticus. If a vote on a particular region is tied, the four leaders use the people in power of the area it would effect the most as a tie breaker vote.
