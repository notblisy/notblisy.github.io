Greywater Swamp

Atmosphere

Description

Area of green and black dragons.

  • Population: Undead, crocodiles, trolls, Crab People
  • Greywater swamp was run by black and green dragons who relentlessly killed humans
  • The two warring clans in this area came together to slay said dragons, and it was the spark of peace for the tribes coming together
  • No one has seen a dragon in a long time
  • People often enter to scavenge potentially old dragon parts for various purposes.
