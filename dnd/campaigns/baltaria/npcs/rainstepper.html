Rainstepper

Introduction

Rainstepper approaches the interview at a snails pace, doesn't blink during the entire interview, and speaks rather slowly. Upon consideration, Rainstepper thanks us, and the vanishes without a trace, with speed we could not have expected.

Description

Rainstepper is an extremely slow moving Tabaxi sorcerer who does not seem to blink. Hired by the PCs to be a part of their guild, H.H.O.G. They went to Flamecrest Academy, are very used to working in the Arctic, and have defended Flamecrest from a White Dragon.
