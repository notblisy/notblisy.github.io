Description

He is a young man of 19, hair down past his shoulders of solid blonde. Easily retaining a flexible figure with combat training. The conflict within himself sometimes escapes into a mood-swing of sorrow or anger, but on every other occasion, he is incredibly nonchalant and happy to be around people. Growing up, if Forebrand didn't have such a close and loving sister, it would be no surprise for her to end up incredibly distant from her family. Katz quickly became his most invested relationship and the thought of losing more family would never escape him as he clings to and protects Her. He trusts people who are kind to him without giving it too much thought. He has trouble thinking before acting and causing undesirable outcomes.
