Feldspar

Atmosphere

Empty and dusty, feldspar may seem like a sad place at first, but you'll soon upon entry learn that every single person in this town is full of life just trying to do their best in a rough situation. It's got much positive atmosphere, and even at its worst when no-one in the town could make any money because One-Eyed-Jack was preventing them from using the mine, everyone kept their chins-up.

Description

Feldspar is the starting town of the adventure. Currently having bandit problems, and the party is hired to deal with them. It is a mining town.

Fairly small population, not more than a few hundred.

Mining export town, back at it since one eyed jack was removed from the mine.

Has a clock tower

The place where the Sanguine Knight was spawned.
