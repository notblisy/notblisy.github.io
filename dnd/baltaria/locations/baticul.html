Baticul

Atmosphere

The merchants district is a distinctly fun place in this town, as you really can't find as many trinkets and objects in one place like you can in Baticul. From a bonestore to a cookwares store, you can find anything from any culture here. Even someone to buy your Giants Cookbook. It's the melting pot of Baltaria.

Description

Baticul is the capital of baltaria and a huge tiered city. The upper most tiers contain the ruling class and the diplomatic courts. The lower tiers contain a mercantile district and a living district.

  • Population: 20,000+ population of all races.
  • Economy: City runs on lots of merchant business, but also has a steady stream of income from all the jobs of people who popped up as service industry to serve the politicians while they stay in the city.
  • Politics: The four leaders of the country preside over the cities courts, meeting with various other people of power to work on adjudicating issues.
  • Culture: It's a multicultural city on account of having to have representatives of all parts of the continent here.
  • Description: 3 tiered city
