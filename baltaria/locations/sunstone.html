Sunstone

Atmosphere

The party teleports in, and as they do, the city appears to be in ruin. What was a fairly well constructed and beautiful desert city had been laid partially to ruin by the Sanguine Knight, the first display anyones seen of its awesome power. As we explored this facsimile of what the city once was, we find an old friend to help us find our way.
