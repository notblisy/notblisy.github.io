The Colonel

Stories

Description

An eccentric socialite from Cobbleston.
Large Giff(Hippo-man)
Loves hunting, extremely friendly and sociable.
Fairly knowledgeable about the history of Baltaria, having crucial knowledge on what the shards were and their role in the history of this world.

Player Character Interaction

The Colonel is a large Giff man. He welcomed us into his home upon first entry into Cobbleston. Having aided us in many ways during our adventures, the most notable would be when he joined us in battle during our attempt to find Hardy and the cult of the iron gear. He wielded a gun and fought off four iron golems with us, nearly losing his life.
